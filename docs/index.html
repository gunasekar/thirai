Thirai turns a home-screen widget into one-tap shortcuts to your shows on the TV — no menus, no remote, no smart-TV navigation. For anyone who wants their shows a tap away, and for anyone who’d rather not wrestle with apps.

How it works

Setup, once

Everything is on the phone and the TV — no extra hardware, no always-on computer, no developer mode.

01

Point at your shows

Build a show list below, host it (a GitHub gist works great), and make a QR.

02

Scan it in the app

Open Thirai → Show source → Scan QR. The phone now knows the shows.

03

Find the TV

Tap “Find my TV”, pick it from the list, and enter the code it shows. Once.

04

Add the widget

Drop the Thirai widget on the home screen. Every tap plays a show. Done.

Thirai talks to the TV over the built-in Android TV Remote service — the same one your phone’s Google TV remote uses.

Config builder

Build your show list

Add each show, then copy the JSON to your host and make a setup QR. The native-language title is shown to the viewer; the English title is just a label. Each show carries its own app package and home URL, so one list can mix apps (Hotstar, Netflix, Prime…).
